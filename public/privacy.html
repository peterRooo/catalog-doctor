Catalog Doctor Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

Catalog Doctor is a Shopify app that helps merchants identify catalog quality issues such as missing images, incomplete SEO fields, pricing mistakes, and empty collections.

Information We Access

Catalog Doctor requests read-only access to product, variant, image, SEO, pricing, and collection metadata from the merchant's Shopify store. This information is used only to generate catalog health reports inside Shopify Admin.

Information We Do Not Access

Catalog Doctor does not request customer data, payment data, checkout data, order data, marketing consent data, or theme files in the current version.

Data Writes

Catalog Doctor does not automatically edit products, collections, inventory, storefront code, checkout, or themes. The app provides issue reports and suggested fixes for the merchant to review.

Data Storage

Catalog Doctor stores Shopify app session information required to keep the app installed and authorized. Catalog scan results are generated on demand and are not sold to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell merchant data. We do not share merchant catalog data with advertisers or data brokers.

Uninstall

When a merchant uninstalls Catalog Doctor, the app receives Shopify's uninstall webhook and removes the stored Shopify session for that shop.
